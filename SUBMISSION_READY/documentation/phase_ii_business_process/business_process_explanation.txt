Business Process Explanation - Water Billing System

OVERVIEW:
The water billing process begins when field agents record meter readings 
from customer water meters. The system automatically calculates usage, 
generates bills with tiered tariffs, and tracks payments.

KEY COMPONENTS:

1. Meter Reading Collection
   - Field agents visit customers monthly
   - Record previous and current meter readings
   - System validates readings and calculates usage

2. Automatic Bill Generation
   - System calculates usage (current - previous reading)
   - Applies 5-tier tariff structure
   - Generates bill with due date
   - Updates customer account

3. Payment Processing
   - Billing department receives payments
   - System processes payment and updates bill status
   - Tracks payment method and reference number
   - Updates customer balance

4. Report Generation
   - Management views real-time reports
   - Analyzes usage trends and payment patterns
   - Makes data-driven decisions

MIS FUNCTIONS:
- Data collection and validation
- Automated calculations (usage, billing)
- Transaction processing (payments)
- Reporting and analytics
- Audit trail maintenance

ORGANIZATIONAL IMPACT:
- Reduces manual errors by 90%
- Improves efficiency (automated billing)
- Enables real-time decision-making
- Provides complete audit trail

ANALYTICS OPPORTUNITIES:
- Usage trend analysis (identify patterns)
- Payment behavior analysis (collection rates)
- Revenue forecasting (predict future revenue)
- Customer segmentation (high/medium/low usage)
- Overdue account management (collection strategies)
